How to Play

Rules:

  1. When the game is started a CS Question is presented, the player is represented with an empty field for each letter for the answer.
  2. When the player guesses a letter correctly, each field that represents that letter is filled with the letter
  3. When the player guesses a letter incorrectly, a piece of a gallow with a hanging man is drawn.
  4. After 10 incorrect guesses, the game is over and the player lost. Thus, there should be 10 different states of the gallow to be drawn.
  5. If all fields are filled with their letter before 10 incorrect guesses, the player has won the game.
